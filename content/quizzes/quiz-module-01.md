- **Questions**: 15 multiple choice

- **Topics Covered**:
  - Monolithic vs Modular architectures
  - Over-collateralization principles
  - LTV, Liquidation Threshold, and Health Factor
  - Utilization rates and interest rate curves
  - Oracle mechanics and risks
  - First position best practices
